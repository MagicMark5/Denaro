This is a customizable budget calculator for breaking down expenses by category and managing cash flow.

Features include: 

1) Breakdown of income and expenses with weekly, monthly, and annual tables 

2) Categorization of expenses 

3) Ability to edit names and amounts of income sources or expenditures and have those changes reflected in the budget result

4) A display summary of cash balance and proportion of expenses
